Complete Guide to Zodiac Signs: Western and Chinese Zodiac

Published: April 2026 | Reading time: 8 min

Zodiac signs have fascinated humans for millennia. Whether you take them seriously or just enjoy the fun, knowing your sign is a universal cultural touchstone. Here's everything about both Western and Chinese zodiac systems.

Chinese Zodiac Signs

The Chinese zodiac cycles every 12 years, with each year represented by an animal. Unlike the Western zodiac (monthly), the Chinese zodiac changes annually at the Lunar New Year.

Chinese Zodiac Elements

Each year also has an element: Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, or Water. These cycle every 60 years (12 animals x 5 elements). The element adds nuance to the animal's traits.
