Chinese Zodiac

The Chinese zodiac operates on a 12-year cycle, with each year associated with an animal: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Pig. Your Chinese zodiac sign is determined by your birth year.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is age calculated?
Age is calculated by finding the difference between your birth date and today's date (or a target date). The calculator accounts for varying month lengths and leap years to give exact results.
How many days old am I?
Enter your birth date and the calculator will show your exact age in days, hours, and minutes in addition to years and months.
How do I calculate the difference between two dates?
Use the Date Difference tab. Enter any two dates and the calculator will show the exact difference in years, months, days, and total days.
When is my next birthday?
Enter your birth date and the calculator automatically shows how many days until your next birthday, along with what day of the week it falls on.
What is my zodiac sign?
Enter your birth date and the calculator displays both your Western zodiac sign (based on month and day) and Chinese zodiac sign (based on birth year).
Does the calculator account for leap years?
Yes, the calculator fully accounts for leap years when calculating age and date differences.
Can I calculate age for someone born in a different timezone?
The calculator uses your local date. For most purposes this is accurate. For legal or official age calculations, use the timezone and exact time of birth if required.
Is this age calculator free?
Yes, this age calculator is completely free with no registration required. All calculations happen in your browser — no data is sent to any server.
